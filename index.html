Bio

Originally from Liaoning, China, Celestine (Liangzi) Liu is a professional vocalist. She obtained her Bachelor of Arts in Music Performance (Folk) at Shenyang Conservatory of Music, and is currently pursuing her master's degree in vocal performance (Bel Canto) at California State University, Northridge. She has numerous performing experiences and is the vocal solo of many famous operas. She is also talented in emceeing and has hosted many galas and festivals. Her performance has made a significant impact on the public and received widespread media coverage.
